### 2. **Add Punctuation for Natural Pauses**

- **Comma (,)** ‚Üí 250ms pause  
- **Question mark (?) or Exclamation (!)** ‚Üí 500ms pause

Example:
```

```

### 3. **Write How You Speak**
Think about how you would actually say it in person:
- Use contractions: "I'm", "you're", "we'll" instead of "I am", "you are", "we will"
- Add friendly words: "Hi!", "Thanks", "Great", "Please"
- Keep sentences shorter (15-20 words max)

### 4. **Add Emphasis Where Needed**
Use natural emphasis words:
- "Really appreciate it"
- "Very important"
- "Absolutely love to hear from you"

## ‚ö° Tips for Better Results

1. **Test Your Message**: Read it out loud before sending
2. **Keep It Brief**: 30-60 seconds of speech is ideal
3. **Avoid Special Characters**: Stick to letters, numbers, and punctuation
4. **Use Numbers Naturally**: Write "three PM" instead of "3 PM" for better pronunciation
5. **Spell Out Abbreviations**: "Doctor Smith" instead of "Dr. Smith"
6. **Add Context**: People need to know who's calling and why
7. **Include Next Steps**: Always tell them what to do next
8. **End Positively**: Close with "Thanks", "Have a great day", etc.
